Match the Animal to Habitat

1. Camel lives in the __________.

Correct Answer: Desert

Camels are well-adapted to live in deserts with their ability to store water and withstand high temperatures.

2. Polar bear lives in the __________.

Correct Answer: Arctic

Polar bears are native to the Arctic region and are well-adapted to cold environments.

3. We smell with our __________.

Correct Answer: Nose

The nose contains olfactory receptors that help us detect different smells.

Correct Answer: Legs

Simple Machine Match

5. A seesaw is an example of a __________.

Correct Answer: Lever

A seesaw is a type of lever that uses a fulcrum to balance two sides.

6. A ramp is an example of an __________ plane.

Correct Answer: Inclined

An inclined plane is a flat surface that is tilted, making it easier to move objects up or down.
