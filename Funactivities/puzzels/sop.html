History Quiz

1. Who was the first Prime Minister of India?

Correct Answer: Jawaharlal Nehru

Jawaharlal Nehru served as India's first Prime Minister from 1947 to 1964.

2. In which year did India gain independence?

Correct Answer: 1947

India gained independence from British rule on August 15, 1947.

3. Name a state in South India.

Correct Answer: Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is one of the southern states of India, known for its rich culture and heritage.

4. Which direction is Kashmir located in India?

Correct Answer: North

Kashmir is located in the northernmost part of India, in the Himalayan region.

Guess the Leader

5. I led the Salt March and believed in non-violence. Who am I?

Correct Answer: Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi led the famous Salt March in 1930 and was a strong advocate of non-violence.

6. I was the queen of Jhansi who fought bravely. Who am I?

Correct Answer: Rani Lakshmibai

Rani Lakshmibai was the queen of Jhansi who fought bravely against the British during the Indian Rebellion of 1857.
