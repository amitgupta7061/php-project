Word Search

Write the word that fits the clue.

1. I shine bright in the sky during the day. What am I?

Correct Answer: Sun

The sun is the star at the center of our solar system that provides light and heat during the day.

2. I twinkle at night and rhyme with "car". What am I?

Correct Answer: Star

Stars are celestial bodies that appear as twinkling points of light in the night sky.

Rhyming Riddles

Guess the word that rhymes!

3. I rhyme with "hat" and chase the mouse. I'm a pet that lives in the house.

Correct Answer: Cat

Cats are common household pets known for their hunting instincts, especially for mice.

4. I rhyme with "bed" and I'm the color red. What fruit am I?

Correct Answer: Apple

Jumbled Sentences

Unscramble and write the correct sentence.

5. dog / The / barked / loudly.

Correct Answer: The dog barked loudly.

This is a simple sentence with a subject (The dog) and a verb (barked) with an adverb (loudly).

6. cake / She / a / baked / chocolate.

Correct Answer: She baked a chocolate cake.

This sentence follows the subject-verb-object pattern with an adjective describing the object.
