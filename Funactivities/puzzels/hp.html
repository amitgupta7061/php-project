Famous Leaders

1. I was the first President of the United States. Who am I?

Correct Answer: George Washington

George Washington was the first President of the United States and served from 1789 to 1797.

2. I led India to independence through non-violent resistance. Who am I?

Correct Answer: Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi was a leader of the Indian independence movement and is known for his philosophy of non-violent resistance.

Important Events

3. In what year did World War II end?

Correct Answer: 1945

World War II ended in 1945 after the surrender of Germany and Japan.

4. When did humans first land on the moon?

Correct Answer: 1969

The first moon landing occurred on July 20, 1969, during the Apollo 11 mission.

Historical Places

5. I am one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, located in Egypt. What am I?

Correct Answer: The Great Pyramid of Giza

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in the Giza pyramid complex.

6. I am a famous wall in China built to protect against invasions. What am I?

Correct Answer: The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications built along the northern borders of China to protect against invasions.
