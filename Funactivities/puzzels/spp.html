1. What equipment is used to hit the ball in cricket?

Correct Answer: Bat

A cricket bat is used by the batsman to hit the ball in the game of cricket.

Team Sport Match

3. Which sport is played with 11 players per team and a goalpost?

Correct Answer: Football

Football is played with 11 players on each team, and the objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opponent's goalpost.

4. Which game is played with a bat, ball, and two teams?

Correct Answer: Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of 11 players each.
