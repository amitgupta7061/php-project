Fitness

Stay Strong and Active! 💪🏃‍♂️

Learn fun ways to keep your body strong and healthy through exercise and movement.

🏃‍♂️

Running

Running is a great way to make your heart strong and keep your body fit.

  • Start with short distances
  • Wear comfortable shoes
  • Drink water after running
🦘

Jumping

Jumping helps make your legs strong and improves your balance.

  • Jump rope for fun
  • Try jumping jacks
  • Play hopscotch
🤸‍♂️

Stretching

Stretching helps your muscles stay flexible and prevents injuries.

  • Stretch before exercise
  • Hold each stretch for 10 seconds
  • Breathe while stretching
💪

Strength Exercises

Simple exercises help build strong muscles and bones.

  • Do push-ups and sit-ups
  • Try squats and lunges
  • Use your body weight
