Healthy Living

Stay Healthy and Happy! 🍎💪

Learn how to take care of your body and mind with simple healthy habits.

🍎

Healthy Eating

Eat colorful fruits and vegetables to keep your body strong and healthy.

  • Eat fruits and vegetables every day
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Choose whole grains
😴

Good Sleep

Get enough sleep to help your body grow and your brain learn better.

  • Sleep 8-10 hours each night
  • Have a regular bedtime
  • Keep your room dark and quiet
🧼

Personal Hygiene

Keep clean to stay healthy and feel good about yourself.

  • Wash your hands often
  • Brush your teeth twice a day
  • Take regular baths or showers
🧠

Mental Health

Take care of your feelings and thoughts to stay happy and focused.

  • Talk about your feelings
  • Take breaks when needed
  • Do things you enjoy
