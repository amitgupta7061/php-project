Safety in Sports

Play Safe, Stay Strong! 🛡️💪

Learn important safety rules to protect yourself and others while playing sports.

🔥

Warm-Up

Always warm up before playing to prevent injuries and prepare your body.

  • Stretch your muscles gently
  • Start with light exercises
  • Gradually increase intensity
🛡️

Safety Equipment

Wear the right gear to protect yourself while playing sports.

  • Always wear helmets when needed
  • Use proper shoes for each sport
  • Wear protective padding
📜

Game Rules

Follow the rules to keep everyone safe and have fun.

  • Learn and follow game rules
  • Play fair and respect others
  • Listen to coaches and referees
🏥

First Aid

Know what to do if someone gets hurt while playing.

  • Tell an adult if someone is hurt
  • Stay calm and help others
  • Know where first aid kits are
